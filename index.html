Our Origin

Where the Western Ghats Shape the Climate.

Our orchard sits in Marappagoundenpudhur, in the mist-touched foothills of the Anaimalai Hills near Pollachi. The Western Ghats regulate temperature, rainfall, and soil moisture — conditions that cannot be replicated elsewhere.

We are a family stewardship, not a commercial operation. Every season is guided by one discipline: grow only what we would serve at our own table. No chemicals. No artificial ripening. No shortcuts.

The Method

The Method Behind the Fruit

Every step protects flavour, texture, and traceability — from the soil to your table.

  1. Soil Stewardship

    Organic compost, seasonal mulching, and soil-rest cycles keep root systems deep and healthy. No synthetic fertilizers touch this ground.

  2. Tree Ripening Only

    Fruit stays on the branch until natural maturity — until the tree releases it. No calcium carbide. No ethylene gas. Just patience.

  3. Hand Harvest

    Each mango is picked by hand with stem intact to minimise bruising and preserve shelf life. Small batches, not bulk clearing.

  4. Direct Dispatch

    Harvest to shipment within hours, not days. Clean packing, careful layering, and direct dispatch to your city. No cold storage, no warehousing.
